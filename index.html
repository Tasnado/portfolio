  • Meal Master Chef

    MMC provides multiple categories for the user to choose from. One they choose a category, they will be given 3 random meals from that category. If they don't like their option, they can keep generating 3 new meals until there are no meals left in the category. If they do like a meal, the user can click on a meal to recieve meal prep instructions and ingredients with an accompanying YouTube video. Worked in pair programming with Hal Forrest.
